INGREDIENTS

  • 1 egg
  • 1 2/3 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup canned pumpkin
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Beat egg in medium bowl. Add milk, pumpkin, butter and vanilla; mix well.
    Mix remaining ingredients in large bowl until well blended.
    Add pumpkin mixture; stir just until blended. Let stand 5 minutes.
  2. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto preheated lightly greased griddle or skillet.
    Cook 1 to 2 minutes per side or until golden brown, turning when pancakes begin to bubble.
